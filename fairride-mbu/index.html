Smart Fare Estimator

Get a fare quote that reacts to live traffic, rider demand, and a transparent breakdown before you confirm.

The 3 Most Important Smart Estimator Features

1. Real-Time Traffic Adjustment

What It Is: We estimate travel time using live routing data and local conditions instead of distance alone.

Why It Matters: Drivers are paid for the time they lose in congestion, and riders understand fare changes during peak hours.

2. Dynamic Demand Surcharging

What It Is: When campus demand spikes, a transparent surcharge appears automatically.

Why It Matters: The surcharge encourages more autos to reach you faster, and we show the exact amount before you book.

3. Interactive Fare Breakdown

What It Is: Tap the estimate to expand a transparent list of every component.

Why It Matters: Trust comes from clarity. You see the base fare, distance, time, traffic, and any surcharge in one place.
